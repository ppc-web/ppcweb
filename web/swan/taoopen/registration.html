Registration

  1. Registration is open to any table tennis players. Registration deadline is 6/20.
    1. Organizers can reserve 18 spots. The rest 90 spots will be given to players according to the time they pay the deposit .
    2. Players need to send $20 deposit to secure their spots. The prefered payment is paypal. The security deposit will be returned to the players when they check in the tournament.
    3. When all the first come first serve 90 spots are taken, the rest players who have paid the deposit will be put on a waiting list. Organizers will contact the players on waiting list if more spots become available.
    4. All security deposit will be returned to players, except for the no show players.
    5. People who have paid deposit and being accepted can cancel their registration before 6/20 to get the deposit returned.
  2. Seeds are determined by USATT tournament rating. Unrated players can ask a coach to give an estimated rating. Organizers will estimate rating for all seeded players who are unrated.
  3. Please fill the online registration form: http://goo.gl/UlE6jf
  4. Please fill in your current USATT tournament rating when registering. Players who fill in wrong USATT rating will be rejected from the tournament once found
  5. Please send your deposit to the following paypal address: pingpong.community@gmail.com
    • Please note the name of the player in your paypal payment.
