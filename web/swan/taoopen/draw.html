Draw

Rules:
  • Group Draw: 96 unseeded players will use the "snake" system to draw into Groups 1-16 according to rating.
    • Minor adjustments may be used to put players of same club into different groups.
    • Odd numbered groups 1, 3, 5, ..., 15 will play at Saturday Morning Session 9am - 2pm.
    • Even numbered groups 2, 4, 6, ..., 16 will play at Saturday Afternoon Session 2pm - 7pm.
    • Group Draw result will be published late Tuesday or Early Wednesday.
    • We will send out the group draw result to all players using the emails they used to sign up.
  • Round of 32: 16 group winners draw 16 group runner ups.
    • Drawing result will be published on Saturday night. Group winner will do the draw if available. Else organizer will do the draw for a group winner.
  • Round of 24 : 8 minor seeds and 16 group advancers will form 12 groups. Minor seeds will be put into 8 different groups. The 16 group advancers randomly draw into the remaining positions.
    • Draw is done immediately after Round of 32.
  • Round of 16 and later:
    • #1 seed is put at position 1.
    • #2 seed is put at position 16.
    • #3 and #4 seeds are randomly drawn into position 8 and 9.
    • The rest 12 players are randomly drawn into the remaining positions.
    • Draw will be done immediately after Round of 24.
  • Cash prize will be given after semi-finals. Same for the raffle.

Result

Results will be published when they are available.
