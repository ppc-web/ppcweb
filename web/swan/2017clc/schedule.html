Preliminary Phase Scheduling

Sept 23 - Nov 9

Weekdays

  1. Two tables are reserved for CLC scheduled games for Noon 12pm - 2pm and Night 7pm - 10pm.
  2. Two matches can be scheduled for four teams. One team can only play one scheduled match.
  3. Please only put your team and your opponent team name in a match slot when both agree to play.
  4. You may use un-reserved table to play a clc match, however, open play policies should be observed. If somebody challenge the table, then the next game must be played with the challenger.
  5. If both scheduled matches are taken, you may still come and play with another team. There is no reserved table for such a match. You will need to follow open table policy to get the match completed.

  1. Specials are set up on Saturday am and pm and Sunday pm with up to 8 tables reserved for CLC games.
