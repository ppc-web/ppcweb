Rules and Format

  1. This is a team competition, primarily for teams from companies, club teams are also accepted. All team types should meet their respective eligibility requirements which are detailed in the eligibility section below.
  2. Each team must be formed with a minimum of 3 players, and a maximum of 6 players. A non-playing captain can be accepted as a 7th member.
  3. Each team match consists of five individual matches and can end when one team has won three out of five matches. Each individual match shall be a best of five games with 11 points in each game.
  4. Before the start of the match, the team that wins the toss shall choose to be either ABC team or XYZ team.
  5. The first player must play two singles. The second player must play one single and one double. No restrictions on other players.
    Order Type ABC team XYZ team
    1 Single A Y
    2 Single B X
    3 Double B + any player except A Y + any player except X
    4 Single A X
    5 Single C Z
  6. The first round will be score based competition. The teams will be divided into two or more groups, teams play every other team in their group. The winning team gets 3 points, and the losing team gets 1 point. Attempt will be made to put teams of same preferred time (lunch time, other time) in same group, otherwise it will be random.
  7. Top teams are decided based on the scores from the first round. Top 14 teams, 7 from each group in case of two groups, will qualify automatic to the playoff round of 16. Winning Ratio will be used as tie breaker.
  8. The remaining 2 spots for the playoff rounds of 16 are decided by the lost pearl qualification events. The teams that did not make the automatic top 14 may register for the lost pearl event. Team selection and format for this qualification event will be posted later.
  9. Playoff round consists of small group round robin + top 8 single elimination.
  10. Top 16 teams will be divided into 4 groups to play round robin based on draws and top 2 teams from each group will be advanced to next round.
  11. Organizers reserve the right to adjust the group size, group drawing, and the play off round size. The adjustments will be done if necessary depending on the number of registered teams for the tournament.

Team Eligibility Requirements

  1. All members of a company team must be employees, interns, or contractors with badge as of registration date.
  2. All members of a club team must be active members or donors of their respective clubs during the entire tournament period.
  3. For club teams, top 2 members cannot exceed a combined USATT rating of 4300 and top 3 members cannot exceed USATT rating of 6200 as of registration start date. Rating is calculated as Max(USATT Tournament Rating, USATT League Rating). Additionally, a player cannot be part of a club team, if there is a company team from the player’s company.
  4. Joint company teams of 2 companies are accepted, provided there are no other individual teams from their respective companies.
  5. Captain of the team is responsible for the integrity of the team, ensure the eligibility of team members, and produce verifiable proofs if demanded by the organizing committee.
  6. Not meeting the eligibility requirements listed above would result in disqualification.
