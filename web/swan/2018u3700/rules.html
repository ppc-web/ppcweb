Eligibility

  1. Any two Ping Pong players can register a team.
  2. The rating sum of two players cannot exceed 3700. If a player has both USATT tournament rating and league rating, use the most recent one. Rating cut off date is 1/13/2018. Organizers can assign a rating to unrated players, or request unrated players to submit professional coach’s letter.
  3. Rating check at https://www.teamusa.org/USA-Table-Tennis/Ratings/USATT-Individuals-Ratings
  4. Former Swan U3200 and U3700 teams can qualify without checking ratings.
  5. Organizers reserve the right to approve players’ rating and eligibility.
  6. Tournament size limits to 42 teams
  7. Registration fee: $30 per player. Register at https://goo.gl/UE6ASt

Format

  1. U3700 is divided into a preliminary phase and a final phase.
  2. In the preliminary phase, teams are randomly drew into groups and play round robin. （Please specify your availability when you register. Organizer will try to consider it) Each group contains around 5 teams and top 2 teams can advance to final round. All the rest teams may register for lost pearl events to qualify for the rest final spots.
  3. Top 16 teams will play group round robin and determine top 8 teams. After that, play single elimination to the end.
  4. A team match consists of 2 single matches and 1 double match. A match is best of 5 games, 11 points per game.
  5. Organizers reserve the right to adjust the group drawing result.
  6. If a team match cannot finish on the day of group RR, the two teams can reschedule the match if both agree. The match must be finished before the end of Mar 4 2018.
