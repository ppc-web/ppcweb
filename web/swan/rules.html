What makes you a good Swan Ping Pong citizen?

  • Be friendly to everyone and enjoy table tennis plays together.
  • Take good care of our tables and equipments and maintain great environment for everyone.
  • Do not move any tables and equipments without permission.
  • Do not put anything on tables. Do not put bags on seats. Please use locker room.
  • Wear table tennis shoes for play ground. Do not use bags or cages with wheels.
  • Drinking is okay in play area, but only eat food in rest area.
  • Do not leave anything in locker room overnight.
  • Stay on time with scheduled classes. Respect our coaches. Notify us 3 hours before changing time.
  • Show up at reserved time. Cancel a reservation 3 hours ahead.
