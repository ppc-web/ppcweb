Beginner/Intermediate Kid Group Class

Sept 11 - Nov 2

  • Four sessions are offered:
    • Tuesday Night: 7 - 9 pm
    • Wednesday Night: 7 - 9 pm
    • Sunday Afternoon: 3 - 5 pm
    • Sunday Night: 6 - 8 pm
  • Each session has 8 lessons. Each lesson is 2 hours.
  • Price: $240

Class Content

This class aims to teach the fundamentals of table tennis to provide kids a solid start in table tennis.

Class contents include but are not limited to:

  • Forehand and backhand strokes
  • Footwork
  • Forehand and backhand rally practices
  • Serving and Receiving
  • Physical training and group games

Coaching

  • Coach Gaolin Tang: former Sichuan provincial team head coach
  • Coach James Miao: former Jilin provincial team member
  • Assistant coaches will be provided for rallying practice with a student/coach ratio of 1:3
