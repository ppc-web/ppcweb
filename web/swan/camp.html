2016 Swan Summer Camp

June 13 - August 26

  • Every weekday from 6/13 to 8/26 from 9:00am - 5:00pm
  • Free Extended Hour up to 6:30pm
  • Bring your own lunch or add $5 for lunch per day

Warrior Sport Camp

  • Dedicated Ping Pong camp for beginner and intermediate level kids
  • 6 hour training every day from 9am - 12am and 2pm - 5pm
  • $390 per Week or $200 per half day week

Discovery Camp

  • 3 hour Ping Pong training plus 1 hour of fun Ping Pong activities
  • 1 hour of Chinese reading/culture (11am-12pm)
  • 1 hour of self paced math learning (2-3pm Afficient Math )
  • $260 per week or $150 per half day week

Discount

  • Loyalty: $20 per week or $10 per half day week if sign up for 3 weeks or more
