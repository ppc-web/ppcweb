Book Drive

Happy New Year, everyone! We are seniors from Irvington High School, and we will be doing a book drive from January 17th to January 31st. All of these books will be going to the Swan Library Corner.
The Covid-19 pandemic has taken a toll on students. From online classes till after school, they are constantly on their phones or laptops. The limitations placed on social gatherings and outside activities have severely restricted the activities available to students in their free time, leading to a sharp increase in screen time by students in the past year. Our book drive is designed to help students find other hobbies! If you have any unwanted books at home, feel free to donate at the following locations and help decrease technology addiction.
You can donate any books that you no longer want at the following locations:

  • Swan Ping Pong
  • O. N. Hirsch Elementary School

    • Your safety during the pandemic is very important to us, so we will set up boxes at these locations to avoid direct contact.

    Any donation is appreciated! Thank you so much!
