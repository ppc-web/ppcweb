history

U3xxx two person team competition is a traditional team events at the swan club. Previously Swan had organized U3200, U3550 competitions. The events used to take nominal fee of $5 to cover prize: Nittaku premium plastic balls.

2017 PPC U3700 Charitable Team Competition

The event this year has changed to be charitable. $25 per person donation is suggested for participation. The donation will be used to support PPC to provide free or low cost facility and education programs to general public.
