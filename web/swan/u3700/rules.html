Eligibility

  1. Any two Ping Pong players can register a team.
  2. The rating sum of two players cannot exceed 3700. If a player has both USATT tournament rating and league rating, use the later rating before Dec 15 2016. Organizers can assign a rating to unrated players.
  3. Players need partner can put your name in the registration table https://goo.gl/56GdXi. Organizers may help you find a partner.

Format

  1. U3700 is divided into a preliminary phase and a final phase.
  2. In the preliminary phase, teams are randomly drew into groups and play round roubin. Each group contains 4-5 teams. The top 2 teams in each group advance to final.
  3. Top 16 teams will play single elimination at the final phase. Teams that have not qualified at the prelimary phase may register for lost pearl events to qualify for the rest final spots.
  4. A team match consists of either 4 single matches and 1 double match, or 2 single matches and 1 double match. A match is best of 5 games, 11 points per game.
    1. For a group RR with 4 teams, a team match is 4 singles and 1 double. First team win 3 matches win.
    2. For a group RR with 5 teams. a team match is 2 singles and 1 double. All three matches must be played.
    3. Final SE will use 5 match format for the first two rounds, and 3 match format for semi-finals and final.
  5. Organizers reserve the right to adjust the group drawing result.
  6. If a team match cannot finish on the day of group RR, the two teams can reschedule the match if both agree. The match must be finished before the end of Jan. 2017.
