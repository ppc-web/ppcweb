PPC Swan Ping Pong Club

Ping Pong Community is the leading nonprofit 501(c)3 organization, a warm home for all levels and ages table tennis players - by connecting you with opportunities to enjoy table tennis and make friends here. We offer table tennis training programs and also open play.
