Swan Open Play Begins on 9/14

Santa Clara county has moved into Red-Tier since Sept 8. Gyms are allowed to open indoor at 10% capacity. We are glad to reopen Swan for open play, with following protocols as Red-Tier restrictions are in place:

  1. Open play time: 12pm - 9pm daily
  2. Maximum capacity / number of open play players: 14.
  3. Open play players can use the following Google Sheet https://tinyurl.com/y22akjul to reserve their play time before coming to Swan. When maximum capacity is reached, players will be allowed to play according to their order on the reservation form. Extra players shall wait outside of the building.
  4. Maximum play time for a player is 2 hours per day.
  5. Players who have played more than 2 hours will be asked to leave when maximum capacity is reached.
  6. Players must wear a mask to be admitted and must wear a mask at any non-play time.

NOTICE:
  1. Players with symptoms such as Fever or Coughing will NOT be admitted.
  2. Players must stay 6 feet away from each other at ALL times.
  3. Players shall wash or sanitize their hands often. Hand sanitizers will be provided.
  4. Players are NOT recommended to change sides.
