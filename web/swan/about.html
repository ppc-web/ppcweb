History

Swan Ping Pong Club was estalished by Haiping Zhao and Amy Zhong on Sept 2012. From the begining, Swan aims to provide the best equipment, the best environment and the best location to Ping Pong players. It soon attracted a large group of fan base. However, high rent at the center of Silicon Valley is always a forbiddable obstacle to overcome for the club.

On Sept 2015, Haiping and Amy permenantly moved back to China, and the club temporarily closed for a month. Then, Mrs Jing Wang bravely took the task to be the professional manager of the club. While in the back, Haiping still provides a large subsidy for the rent, Mrs Wang has been working extremely hard for no pay to continue operating the club and get the rest of rent paid.

Future

Due to family issues, Mrs Wang is unable to manage the club after the end of April 2016. The club faces the risk of closing again.

We, a group of Ping Pong fans, are stepping up to prevent this from happening. We are establishing a 501(c)(3) public charity organization: Ping Pong Community, to continue managing and operating the Swan club.

Our belief is a high-end Ping Pong club deserves to survive and profit, though it may be a long way to go. Before that happens, Ping Pong fans can step up and take the financial risks and managerial burdens, so that professional coaches can focus on teaching. We need both the players and professional coaches to work together to make this happen.

We have a dream, and let's make it happen.

Special Thanks

To appreciate Mrs Jing Wang's contribution to the Swan Club, she will be the permanent VIP member while PPC is managing the Swan Club.
