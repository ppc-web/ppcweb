2016 Company/Club League Competition

2016 CLC allows joint teams and club teams to compete for the CLC title and prize.
This CLC is also the first one to collect entry fees and offer prize award.
We may make necessary adjustments in the rules in following CLCs to make CLC a regular and more impactful sport event.
