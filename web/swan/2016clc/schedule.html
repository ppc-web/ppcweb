Latest Big RR Schedule

Preliminary Phase: 8/13 - 10/26

In the Preliminary big RR phase, Teams can schedule the matches themselves, or participate in Swan Special events. In a Swan Special Event, the teams just need to be there. Any teams can play with each other if both agree to play and have not played before. 8 Tables will be reserved for the CLC matches.

Play Off: 11/5 - 11/12

The top 16 teams of the Big RR phase advance to the play off stage. They will draw randomly into 4 groups. Play off will be played on two Saturdays, Nov 5th and Nov 12th.

Play off Group RR: Nov 5th, Saturday.

On the Nov 5th, the four groups will play group round robin. The top 2 teams of each group advance to the final 8.

Play off final single elimination: Nov. 12th Saturday.

On Nov 12th, the final 8 will play single elimination to decide the CLC Champion and runner up.

For the CLC final on Nov 12th, Swan club will rearrange to have only 4-5 tables. Spectator seats will be set up. The games will be lively broadcasted. There will be media coverage. We will do our best to make the game a high level competing and watching experience.
