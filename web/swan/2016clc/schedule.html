Latest Big RR Schedule

Preliminary Big RR Phase Schedule

In the Preliminary big RR phase, Captains can schedule the matches themselves, or participate in Swan Special events. In a Swan Special Event, the teams just need to be there. Any teams can play with each other if both agree to play and have not played before. Tables will be reserved for the CLC matches.

Play Off Schedule

The top 16 teams of the Big RR phase advance to the play off stage. They will draw randomly into 4 groups. Play off will be played on two Saturdays. On the first Saturday, each group will play round robin and the top 2 teams advance to the final 8. On the second Saturday, the final 8 will play single elimination to decide the CLC Champion and runner up.

On the second Saturday, Swan club will rearrange to have only 4-5 tables. Spectator seats will be setup. The games will be lively broadcasted. There will be media coverage. We will will do our best to make the game a high level competing and watching experience.

The play off date has not been finalized yet. The time frame is from end of Oct. to beginning of Nov. We are coordinating with Oriental Pearl Cup to make sure there is no conflict. Stay tuned.
