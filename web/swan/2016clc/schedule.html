Latest Big RR Schedule

Preliminary Phase: 8/13 - 10/26

In the Preliminary big RR phase, Teams can schedule the matches themselves, or participate in Swan Special events. In a Swan Special Event, the teams just need to be there. Any teams can play with each other if both agree to play and have not played before. 8 Tables will be reserved for the CLC matches.

Play Off Group RR: 11/5

The top 16 teams of the Big RR phase advance to the play off stage. They will draw randomly into 4 groups and play Round Robin on 11/5. Top two teams of each group advance to final.

Drawing detail:

  • Use https://www.random.org/lists/?mode=advanced for drawing
  • Top 16 teams are initially ordered according to their preliminary phase points as of Friday 10/28 1pm.
  • Use Friday, 10/28's Dow closing index as the seed
  • 1-4 will be assigned to Group A, 5-8: B, 9-12: C and 13-16: D
  • Draw will be final at 10/28 1pm. After that, score can still be corrected but won't affect drawing, except any new teams make into top 16 will replace the teams drop out of top 16 in the draw.
  • Draw will be published Friday 3pm

On Nov 12th, the final 8 will play single elimination to decide the CLC Champion and runner up. The CLC champion will also play against Ohlone College for the Bonus Champion.

For the CLC final on Nov 12th, Swan club will rearrange to have only 4-5 tables. Spectator seats will be set up. The games will be lively broadcasted. We will do our best to make the game a high level competing and watching experience.

A2 and B2, the second places of Group A and B, will be randomly drawn to play C1 and D1. Visa Versa for C2 and D2.

The same random drawing process is used except the seed will be Dow's closing index on Monday 11/7 for A2 and B2, and Nasdaq's closing index for C2 and D2. The draw will be published on night of 11/7.

Final single elimination starts at 1pm.

CLC Champion will play Ohlone College for the Bonus Championship and prize of $200 at the end.
