Benefits

General

  • PPC volunteers include directors, officers and volunteer workers.
  • PPC volunteers still need to pay annual membership fee in general.
  • PPC volunteers automatically get VIP membership after paying personal annual membership fee.

VIP membership benefit

  • Free open play for family members
  • Can bring one guest for free open play any day
  • Have a reserved locker
  • Free usage of any fee-based equipments, such as Ping Pong robot
  • Can have a free party every 3 months.
  • Party rules: 15 people or below. 3 hours. Reserve up to 3 tables. Reserve one week in advance. Cannot overlap with peak hours (Weekday 12-2 and League time).

Membership Exception

  • Volunteer coaches do not need to pay annual membership fee
  • Senior or low income volunteers do not need to pay annual membership fee
