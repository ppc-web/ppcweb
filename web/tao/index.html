Swan Warriors Table Tennis Center

Swan Warriors is founded on May 2017 by Wenzhang Tao, 2014 US Open Table Tennis Single Champion.

We aim to build the best Ping Pong School and Club for young players to become US, North American or International Champions.

We are organizing the Tao Swan Pre-National Open tournament on June 24 and 25. The tournament has $5,000 prize award and is open to all. Join us for the fun!
