PPC open Swan free to public

Dec 6 2016

Starting from Dec. 6, 2016, open play at Swan Ping Pong club is free to general public.

  • Free Kid Group Class

    Dec. 2016

    PPC starts to offer free kid group classes daily Mon - Thurs at Swan Club. Each group class is one hour long and can accommodate up to 10 students.

  • PPC is organizing SWAN winter Camp

    12/19 - 12/30

    PPC will hold Ping Pong Winter Camp at Swan from 12/19 - 12/30.
