Ping Pong Community is incorporating

Mission

We are establishing PPC as a 501(c3) public charitable organization to promote the sport of Ping Pong. Our missions seek to:

  • Open, operate or sponsor Ping Pong facilities/clubs in local communities for Ping Pong players to play the sport, and for kids to learn the sport.
  • Support and sponsor Ping Pong programs in local schools.
  • Support and sponsor talented kids.
  • Promote Ping Pong in cooperation by opening company Ping Pong clubs and organizing intra-company and inter-company competitions.

Plans

  • ONGOING Nov 2015 : Draft missions, articles of incorporation and bylaws. Recruit initial board of directors and officials. Get initial funding. Setting up web server.
  • Dec 2015: Register NPO organization. Apply for tax exempt status. Setting up accounting system.
  • Q1 2016: Get the 501(c3) status approved by government. Start to take public donations. Open the first part time Ping Pong facility in a local community. Open the first company Ping Pong club.
