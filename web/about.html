Mission

PPC is a 501(c)(3) public charity. Our mission is to improve the physical and mental health of public by practicing and learning the sport of Ping Pong. Our activities include but are not limited to the following:

  • Provide free or low cost Ping Pong facilities for public to play the sport of Ping Pong.
  • Provide educational Ping Pong programs for public to learn the sport.
  • Donate Ping Pong equipments to local schools. Provide free Ping Pong classes at local schools.
  • Organize Ping Pong competitions.

Current Charitable Activities

  • Provide free Ping Pong play to general public at Swan Ping Pong Club .
  • Provide daily Free Kid beginner group classes.
  • Donate at least two Ping Pong tables to a local school each quarter.
  • Provide a free two hour group class at a local school at least once a month.
  • Help local schools to build Ping Pong program. Offer free training of School teams at Swan Club. Establish inter-school league competition.
