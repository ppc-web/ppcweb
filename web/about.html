Ping Pong Community is incorporating

Mission

We are establishing PPC as a 501(c3) public charitable organization to promote the sport of Ping Pong (Table Tennis) and therefore, improve the physical and mental health of public. Our missions seek to:

  • Open and operate Ping Pong facilities/clubs in local communities where commercial Ping Pong clubs have difficulty to survive.
  • Support and sponsor Ping Pong programs in local schools.
  • Support and sponsor talented kids by providing scholarship, coaching and travel assistance.
  • Organize Ping Pong competitions.
  • Help establishing company Ping Pong clubs and organizing inter company competitions.
